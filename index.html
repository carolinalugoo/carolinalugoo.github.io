About Me

Hi! My name is Carolina Lugo and I'm currently a student at the University of Miami. Last semester I had the priveledge of studying abroad in Paris. During the semester I made some of my best friends and got to visit the most increadible places.

Paris

My home base was the city of lights Paris, France. I have so much love for Paris and the experiences and memories that the city gave me. I lived in 20th arrondissement with my roommate Aylin who is also a student at the University of Miami!

Milan

Italy was the first place we traveled to alone to and it was one my favorite cities that I got to visit. We visited some iconic places in Milan such as:

  • The Last Supper Painting
  • Piazza del Duomo
  • Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II

Belgium

Although short our time in Belgium sure was sweet. We got walk around the gorgeous city, try belgian waffles and fries which are a must, and go into a chocolate factory for a test tasting!
